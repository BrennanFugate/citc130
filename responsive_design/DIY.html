Tabletop RPGs

Sci-Fi RPGs Such As:

  • Alien
  • Call of Cthulhu
  • Cyberpunk Red
  • Lancer
  • Shadowrun
  • Spelljammer - A D&D Spin-Off
  • Starfinder
  • One of the FOUR Star Wars RPGs

Each of these games offer a fantastic entry point into sci-fi writing by immersing players in richly detailed worlds that either take place in, or are reminiscent of everyone's favorite science fiction universes. Tabletop roleplaying games provide a structured yet flexible narrative framework, allowing writers to experiment with world-building, character development, and thematic exploration in real-time, with friends. By crafting dialogue, making decisions, and facing challenges, players engage with sci-fi's core elements—such as advanced technology, alien cultures, and ethical dilemmas—making these RPGs a dynamic springboard for storytelling. The collaborative nature of RPGs also fosters creativity, as players exchange ideas build on each other's contributions, and organically develop complex plots, offering a hands-on approach to the science fiction genre.
